BET - Workout Motivation Application

About

BET is an application purposed for motivating busy workers to exercise throughout their daily lives, and it provides diverse challenges to allow users to work out more easily in the midst of their daily routines. Users can be more motivated to complete working out by betting points against the challenges provided.

Feedback from users

"We received feedback regarding creating a challenge for users as well as how this can cause them inconvenience, so the Bet app will automatically set daily or weekly challenges, including recommended courses which allow users to participate in a quick and simple way without having to create a challenge."

The second iteration

After we developed the high-fidelity prototype, we engaged in additional user testing with 3 users.

For efficient user testing, we observed how users interacted with our product by means of screen sharing on Zoom and receiving feedback from them.

Good things

1. All three potential users we interviewed were positive regarding the idea of betting.

2. The application is generally neat and easy to understand.

3. Mission clear is likely to motivate more in the process of conducting the challenge.

Feedback 1

1. The wide range of timeframes to complete the challenge makes individuals rather unmotivated and less likely to exercise each day.

2. Hard to catch what the number of points indicates at a glance.

3. On the challenge details page, you want to know how many miles, how many missions, and how long the estimated time will take. Additionally, I would like to determine the difficulty level (easy, normal, hard) for each challenge.

Feedback 2

1. I would like to see how many challenges I completed on the My Bet page.

2. I would like to make my own challenges with a course myself. (Next Step)

‍Explore challenges

Users can participate in a challenge by filtering the date or location they desire. In addition, the more users participate, the greater the bet amount.

Participate in a challenge

After betting a certain amount of money, users can begin the challenge.

Work out and complete missions

During the challenge, the map tracks the user's location accurately. Carry out the mission in the middle. Sometimes, users can receive bonus points.

Check their challenges status

Users can check the schedule and see to what degree they have accomplished the challenges and missions.

Transfer your points to your bank account

Users can easily transfer collected points into their bank account and check their history. The payment methods can be modified on the setting page.

Takeaways & Next Steps

Takeaways

New insights from user testing

We obtained diverse ideas and feedback from a new perspective through user testing with three individuals. I was able to distill the information and add additional helpful information by rethinking which information was precious to users. We realized that user testing is one of the most vital processes for designers in that we are able to verify our assumptions and improve our product to be more user-centered.

Next step

Consider a variety of content to motivate users to exercise

During user testing, users stated that it would be great for them to engage in a small challenge where they can create and perform on their own, as well as the challenges recommended by the app. However, for the sake of time, we decided to consider other new features which motivate users to exercise during the next step.
